I am a B.Tech-M.Tech CSE student at **NFSU Gandhinagar** working at the intersection of quantum computing, scientific machine learning, and operator learning.

My current research direction centers on models that respect structure instead of treating physics as generic sequence data. That means sparse geometry-aware attention for PDE operators, qudit circuit constructions beyond qubits, and practical systems that connect theory to implementation.

Currently building:

- **GOLA**: a geometric neural operator with sparse local attention
- **Qutrit Circuit Framework**: tools for simulating and studying higher-dimensional quantum gates
- **Neural Operators for Fluid Systems**: operator-learning pipelines for Navier-Stokes style problems
